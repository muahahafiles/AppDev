About Me

I consider myself to be someone who has always gravitated towards technology, and the process of complex problem solving. My studies originally started with information technology, specifically hardware repair. For as long as I could hold a screw driver, I was tinkering with whatever I could get my hands on. Eventually, I started becoming more interested in things that were less physical, and ways to fix them, which compelled me to become engrossed in the field of psychology, and the way that the innerworkings of our brains interacted with the physical world. Through my exploration of these two fields, I came to the realization that computer science offered a synthesis of these two interests, and I decided to pursue this field in order to create an application of the two that will be of benefit to the world.
